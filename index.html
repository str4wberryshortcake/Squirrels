Squirrels

The Squirrel Census is a multimedia science, design, and storytelling project focusing on the Eastern gray (Sciurus carolinensis). They count squirrels and present their findings to the public.

Throughout their lives, squirrels display impressive behaviors. They are excellent climbers, effortlessly navigating trees with their sharp claws and powerful hind legs. These resourceful creatures construct nests called 'dreys' using leaves, twigs, and other materials, providing cozy shelters high above the ground.
